Prepare


1. You will need headphones. Plug 'em in and put 'em on.

2. This won't work if your phone falls asleep. On an iPhone, go to "Settings → General → Auto-Lock" and temporarily disable Auto-Lock.

3. When you start walking, your phone should be in your right front pocket. If you don't have a pocket, a bag over one shoulder might work.
