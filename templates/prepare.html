Prepare

You will need headphones. Plug 'em in and put 'em on.

This won't work if your phone falls asleep. Go to "Settings → General → Auto-Lock" and temporarily disable Auto-Lock.

When you start walking, your phone should be in your right front pocket. If you don't have a pocket, try a bag over your shoulder. Carrying the phone or putting it in a backpack won't work.
