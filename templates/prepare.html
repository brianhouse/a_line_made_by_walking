Prepare

  • You will need headphones. Plug 'em in and put 'em on.
  • This won't work if your phone falls asleep. Go to "Settings → General → Auto-Lock" and temporarily disable Auto-Lock.
  • When you start walking, your phone should be in your right front pocket. If you don't have a pocket, try a bag over your shoulder. Backpack or just carrying the phone won't work.
