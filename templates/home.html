Synchronizing Uncertainty


Using your phones's accelerometer, this web app will record the rhythm of your footsteps as you walk.

...and while you are walking, you will hear a click track of the footsteps made by someone else who walked before you. Try and match them. Or don't.

After you're done, your walk will guide someone else. And so on and so forth.
